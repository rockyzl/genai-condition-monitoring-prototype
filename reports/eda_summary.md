# FD001 Exploratory Data Analysis

Generated by pipeline stage `s02_eda` (`src/pipeline/eda.py`) from `data/raw/CMAPSSData/train_FD001.txt`. Descriptive only — it justifies the preprocessing and modelling choices made downstream.

## Unit-lifetime distribution

- Units: **100**, training rows: **20631**.
- Lifetime (cycles to failure): min **128**, median **199**, mean **206.3**, max **362**.
- **38.9%** of training rows (8,031 of 20,631) have a true remaining life above the 125-cycle cap and are clipped to it. 38.9% of training rows have a true remaining life above 125 cycles — healthy early life where the sensors barely move. Clipping the target at 125 stops the model fitting noise on that healthy stretch and matches how a maintenance team acts (anything beyond ~125 cycles is simply 'healthy').
